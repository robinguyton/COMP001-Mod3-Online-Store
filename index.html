Mission Statement

At Robin's Machined Customs we approach every new project the same way; and that is to ensure your idea is crafted to the highest possible quality each time.

We have over $1 million worth of high-tech, calibrated machinery that puts out only the most precise products for our customers. We work with contracts from the military, local auto shops, out-of-state automotive companies, and our cherished local customers.

I don't want any of our potential new customers to ever feel intimidated by a new project or an idea that they have, so even if you're not sure what you're looking for, we can guide you from the very beginning!

Happy Accident

I began a love of machining parts when I enlisted into the Navy in January, 2010.
I had no idea that the machinist trade even existed, and I was given this job as a "Needs of the Navy" billet!
Turns out, that was the best thing that ever happened to me!
So in 2024, I decided to create my own company to showcase what this amazing trade is capable of.
It's also a blessing to see other women get encouraged to pursue a career in the trades.
